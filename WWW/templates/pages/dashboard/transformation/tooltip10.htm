Definition:
Combination of stages 1 to 6 in TTD:
Stage 1 in TTD - Time at QDE step (at Branches)
Stage 2 in TTD - Time on-hold at QDE step (at Branches)
Stage 3 in TTD - Time at DDE step (at CPC-UW)
Stage 4 in TTD - Underwriting stage (at CPC UW) - excluded time at Phone verification & Field Investigation step
Stage 5 in TTD - Time at Phone Verification step
Stage 6 in TTD - Time at Field Investigation step

Link to strategy:
This indicator reflects loan origination efficiency. Rapid growth in sales without parallel improvement in processing efficiency will create bottleneck and compromise long-term growth

Limitations:
Short turn around time does not always lead to low cost. Underwriters can distort turn around time by requesting applications to be resubmitted.
