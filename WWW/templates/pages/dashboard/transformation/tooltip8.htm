Definition:
Average number of products per product holder

Link to strategy:
This indicator reflects how effective the bank is selling to its existing customers, which is a less expensive source of profit than new acquisitions. Growing cross-sell capability is also strategic preparation for a future when the ability to grow by new acquisitions significantly diminishes owing to size and market maturity.

Limitations:
Increase product holding does not necessarily means increase in revenue stream as customer can simply shift balance from one account to another without adding to overall revenue or loyalty.
